About Us

Mestizo de Sangley FBO is a proud subsidiary of Codog Aviation, designed to open Philippine skies with inclusive, innovative, and global-standard aviation services. Strategically based at Sangley Point, we provide seamless support for general aviation, VIP travelers, airlines, cargo operators, medevac, and military missions. With nationwide reach and worldwide partnerships, Mestizo de Sangley is redefining what it means to be a modern FBO in the Philippines.
